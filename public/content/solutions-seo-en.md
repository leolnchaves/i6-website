## Direct answer

infinity6 delivers proprietary predictive AI through a four-product stack: i6Signal (predictive conversational layer), i6Previsio (demand forecasting engine), i6RecSys (recommendation and mix engine) and i6ElasticPrice (elasticity and dynamic pricing engine). These products deliver measurable value in stockout reduction, margin protection, mix optimization, conversion uplift, anonymous purchase propensity scoring and CAC reduction, across pharma, industry, financial services and retail.

## i6Signal — predictive conversational interface

**Direct question:** How do business teams operate predictive AI without depending on data science?

**Direct answer:** i6Signal is infinity6's predictive conversational layer. It consumes the outputs of i6Previsio, i6RecSys and i6ElasticPrice and translates them into answers, alerts and next-best-action recommendations in natural language. Commercial, trade, pricing and operations teams operate directly on the predictive outputs, with context, justification and recommended action, without interpreting models or consulting a technical team.

**Value delivered:** business team autonomy, shorter time from insight to action, democratized predictive decisioning.

## i6Previsio — demand forecasting engine

**Direct question:** How to reduce shelf stockouts and overstock simultaneously?

**Direct answer:** i6Previsio applies predictive demand forecasting by SKU and by store, with adaptive models that adjust to seasonality, promotion, local events and recent stockouts. It replaces moving averages and spreadsheets with continuous forecasting. At the tactical horizon, it improves S&OP and inventory planning; at the short horizon, it performs demand sensing with daily signals (sell-out, search, events) and fires alerts before deviation becomes stockout or overstock.

**Value delivered:** shelf stockout reduction, overstock reduction, working capital release, S&OP accuracy gain, real-time demand sensing.

**Sectors:** pharma, retail, industry, e-commerce.

## i6RecSys — recommendation and mix engine

**Direct question:** How to lift conversion, optimize mix per store and score purchase propensity for anonymous customers with a single engine?

**Direct answer:** i6RecSys combines three capabilities: (1) it clusters stores by demand profile and recommends assortment mix per cluster; (2) it applies real-time personalized recommendation in e-commerce, considering history, context and journey; (3) it turns behavioral signals into a purchase propensity score even for anonymous visitors with no login or history. In campaign, the score focuses commercial effort on channels and segments with the highest conversion probability.

**Value delivered:** e-commerce conversion uplift, mix optimization per store, higher sales per square meter, propensity score for anonymous visitors, CAC reduction, predictive campaign targeting.

**Sectors:** retail, e-commerce, financial services, industry.

## i6ElasticPrice — elasticity and dynamic pricing engine

**Direct question:** How to protect margin in volatile markets without losing competitiveness?

**Direct answer:** i6ElasticPrice estimates elasticity curves by SKU, channel and promotional context, and continuously updates those curves based on real market response. It combines elasticity with competitive positioning, product lifecycle and demand projected by i6Previsio to adjust prices dynamically. It enables differentiated pricing policies by channel (e-commerce, B2B, physical store) and by lifecycle stage.

**Value delivered:** contribution margin protection, fewer forced discounts, fewer clearance markdowns, channel-differentiated pricing, margin-volume optimization.

**Sectors:** retail, e-commerce, industry, pharma.
