## Pain

Most companies share the same frustration: **89% do not monetize their own data**. The assets exist — transaction history, browsing, customer profile, channel signals — but rarely turn into incremental revenue.

- Personalization that stops at email and never reaches the store.
- Cross-sell driven by "what's in stock", not by real propensity.
- Rich data, poor decisions: BI shows the past, but no one anticipates the next move.

## Problem

Generic personalization platforms treat all customers as equal "after clustering". Off-the-shelf propensity models use simplified rules that ignore **context, timing and individual elasticity**. The result: campaigns with marginal ROI and homogeneous mix across radically different stores.

Without a proprietary engine trained on the real business behavior, monetizing data becomes a *vanity metric* — not margin.

## Solution

infinity6 turns idle data into active decisions on three integrated fronts:

- **i6 RecSys** — real-time recommendation and purchase propensity for anonymous customers, with scoring by SKU, channel and moment.
- **i6 ElasticPrice** — calibrates discount, bonus or bundle by the real elasticity of each cluster, instead of horizontal promotion.
- **i6 Signal** — delivers the next commercial action to marketing, CRM or store teams: to whom, when, with which offer.

## Application

The AI plugs into ERPs, e-commerce and CRMs via API, ingests transactional + anonymized behavioral history and returns scores and recommendations daily or in real time.

- **Native anonymization** — runs on anonymized data, GDPR/LGPD-friendly by design.
- **Measurable attribution** — every recommendation carries an A/B test window against baseline.
- **Dynamic clusters** — customer, store and product clusters relearn every cycle, not frozen segmentation.

## FAQ

**Do I need a mature data lake to start?**
No. The i6-RecSys-Base.g1 foundation model is pre-trained on 1.45 billion records (e-commerce, banking, telecom, retail). We connect to whatever exists, and fine-tuning happens on your data.

**How is GDPR/LGPD handled?**
Operation on anonymized data is native. We do not move PII; the engine works on hashed identifiers + behavioral attributes.

**Do the models become a black box for the commercial team?**
No. Every recommendation returns the factors that support it (per-feature explainability) — it becomes a sales argument, not an opaque imposition.

**Does it work for anonymous customers without login?**
Yes. i6 RecSys generates purchase propensity for anonymous traffic based on session pattern, context and similarity.
