Traditional demand forecasting based on moving averages no longer captures post-pandemic complexity. Brick-and-mortar competing with e-commerce, broken seasonality and unstable global chains demand models that learn in real time.

## Where predictive AI fits

- Blends internal signals (sell-in, sell-out, inventory) with external ones (weather, events, search)
- Updates forecasts in short cycles (daily/weekly vs. monthly)
- Quantifies uncertainty per SKU/store — not just point estimates
